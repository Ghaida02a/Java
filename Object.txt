1- Computer
	States: running applications, RAM usage, Network connectivity, current power status(on/off). 
	Behaviors: shut down, sleep, connect to the internet, execute programs.

2- Television
	States: volume level, power(on/off), channel.
	Behaviors: play, pause, switch channels, turn on, turn off, adjust volume

3- Mobile Phone
	States:  power status (on, off, restarting), battery level, screen status, Ringer mode(silent, vibrate, loud).
	Behaviors: power (on/off), restart, charge battery, Open/close apps.

4- Printer
	States: power status, paper status, Ink level, Connection status.
	Behaviors: Turn on/off, Copy pages, Load or eject paper, connect to a computer/network.

5- Traffic Light
	States: timer, color.
	Behaviors: change color, Respond to vehicle/pedestrian sensors.

6- Door
	States: open/closed, locked/unlocked.
	Behaviors: open door, close, lock, unlock.

7- Smartwatch
	States: battery level, power status, Notifications(unread/read).
	Behaviors: power on/off, charge battery, check notifications(read/unread).

8- Car
	States: engine status, fuel level, door status.
	Behaviors: start/stop engine, refuel, Lock/unlock doors.

9- Refrigerator
	States: internal temperature, door status, cooling mode, light status.
	Behaviors: cool, freeze, light turns on/off, Display temperature, open/close door.

10- Fan
	States: open/closed, speed level.
	Behaviors: turn on, turn off, change speed level

11- Speaker
	States: power status, volume status, battery level, connection status.
	Behaviors: turn on/off, charge battery, check volume level, check connection.

12- Washing machine
	States: power status, water level, door status, mode selected.
	Behaviors: power on/off, Adjust water level, door open/close,light/heavy wash.

13- Elevator
	States: door status, direction, current floor. 
	Behaviors: move up/down, open/close door, stop at selected floor.

14- Microwave
	States: power status, door status, set timer, light status.
	Behaviors: open/close door, set timer, open/close light

15- Coffee machine
	States: power status, water level, temperature status, Cleaning status.
	Behaviors: Turn on/off, perform cleaning cycle, adjust brew strength, adjust cup size

16- Clock
	States: time, alarm status, sound status.
	Behaviors: alarm, reset, set/cancel an alarm, Snooze alarm.

17- Router
	States: power status, connection status, Wi-Fi signal, device count.
	Behaviors: reboot/reset, connect to the internet, restrict/block devices.

18- Projector
	States: power status, brightness level, focus status.
	Behaviors: power on/off, switch input source, project image/video, display warning.

19- Garage Door
	States: door position, lock status, power status.
	Behaviors: open/close door, lock/unlock door, respond to remote control or smart app.

20- Camera
	States: power status, battery level, focus status, mode.
	Behaviors: turn on/off, zoom in/out, adjust focus, switch between modes, take photos/record video.
